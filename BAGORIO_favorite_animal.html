My Favorite Animal:Dog

My favorite animal is the dog. Dogs are known for their loyalty, intelligence, and affectionate nature. They come in various breeds, sizes, and colors, and are often referred to as "man's best friend" due to their close relationship with humans. Whether it's playing fetch, going for walks, or simply cuddling on the couch, dogs bring joy and companionship to many people's lives.
